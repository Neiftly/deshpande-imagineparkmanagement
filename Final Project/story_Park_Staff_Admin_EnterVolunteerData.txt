Date: 2020-04-07
Description:
	Tells the story of the Park Staff Admin entering
	new Volunteer data.
	Park Staff Administrator will be refered to as Admin
	
	
Basic Flow
1	Admin starts new Volunteer record
2	Admin enters Volunteer name
3	Admin enters Volunteer address (if avaialable)
4	Admin enters Volunteer primary phone number (if avaialable)
5	Admin enters Volunteer email address (if avaialable)
7	Admin enters Volunteer Availability
8	Admin enters Volunteer skillset (if avaialable)
9	Admin enters Volunteer limitations (if avaialable)
10	Admin confirms information
11	Record is complete




Fields Identified or Suggested
Volunteer
	-ID number(auto)
	-Name
	-Address
	-Phone
	-Email
	-Salary(Volunteer Identifier)
	-Availability
	-Skillset
	-Limitations
